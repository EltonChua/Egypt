About Egypt History

Preserving and sharing Egypt's rich heritage with the world

Our Story

Founded in 2010 with a passion for Egypt's extraordinary past

Egypt History was founded by a team of archaeologists, historians, and digital specialists who shared a common vision: to make Egypt's incredible heritage accessible to everyone. What started as a small passion project has grown into a comprehensive resource visited by millions each year.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic research and public interest, presenting Egypt's fascinating history in an engaging, accurate, and visually compelling way. We work with leading Egyptologists and institutions worldwide to ensure our content meets the highest standards of historical accuracy.

Our Mission

To preserve and share the remarkable story of Egyptian civilization through innovative digital experiences that educate, inspire, and connect people across generations and cultures.
